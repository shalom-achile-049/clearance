About the System

The Driving Clearance Report System connects drivers, companies, and government authorities with transparent records to improve road safety and accountability.

For Companies

Employers can evaluate drivers based on mistake history and hire certified, responsible drivers.

  • Evaluate candidates by repeated mistakes
  • Hire drivers with clean records
  • Ensure compliance with safety standards

For Government

Authorities can enforce accountability and rehabilitation for drivers who repeat mistakes.

  • Penalize repeat offenders
  • Offer rehabilitation paths
  • Analyze trends for safer policies

For Drivers

Drivers can access their reports, track history, and clear bad records through rehabilitation.

  • View and download reports
  • Track driving history
  • Correct mistakes officially

Future Capabilities

The system will expand with new features to support companies, governments, and drivers.

  • Integration with hiring platforms
  • Analytics dashboards
  • Driver training modules
  • Mobile apps for quick access
